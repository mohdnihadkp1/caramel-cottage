Our Story

Every cake is a canvas.
Every flavor, a memory.

Nestled in the heart of Kerala, Caramel Cottage is more than a bakery — it's a live cake studio where artistry meets indulgence. Founded with a passion for crafting edible masterpieces, we bring your sweetest visions to life, one layer at a time.

We are proudly women-owned and LGBTQ+ friendly. Our roots run deep in Kerala's rich culture, and every creation reflects the warmth, color, and soul of our homeland.
